Isekai

Ahhh... The classic die and ressurect in a different world experience, the idea is nice and all but its also highly overused with animes where the main character comes back as a fridge or something like that (kinda weird not gonna lie)

There are on the other side, very good animes with this theme, I'll list below my top 3 animes of this genre:

  • Konosuba
  • No game no life
  • Angel Beats

Yes, you didn't read wrong, Angel Beats is a isekai anime that will dehydrate you from how much it will make you cry :D
